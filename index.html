ES6 javascript BMI converter

This is a web of BMI(Body-Mass-Index) converter:

  1. In this web 1st you enter your height in Feet and inch.
  2. Then enter your weight in KG.
  3. After enter all instruction you get your result of your Body-Mass-Index.

    4. e.g=> you are obese, you are underweight or you are normal etc!

BMI Categories:

Underweight = < 18.5
Normal weight = 18.5 – 24.9
Overweight = 25 – 29.9
Obesity = BMI of 30 or greater

  • Healthy BMI range: 18.5 kg/m2- 25 kg/m2
